Evaluation

Our battlebot was overall well produced, as the finished product was as expected to what we designed for.

We first brainstormed the design of it and chose to do a custom build to have full flexibility with the weapon and motor integrations.

This took a lot of extra work, but it really paid off in the visual aspect with the use of CAD modeling tools to help us see exactly what we were working on and set the foundation quite strong.

From there on, we encountered numerous roadblocks, including motor control and wiring.

The constant design refinement, motor repositioning, and simplification of code helped us to identify and iron out these issues.

We managed to work through these issues, managed to fix wiring and voltage issues, and worked on ensuring that the motor's operation was consistent.

Unfortunately due to bad motor the whole bot melted, breaking the acrylic and glue holding some things in place.
